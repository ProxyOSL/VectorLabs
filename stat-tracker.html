What is Stat Tracker Pro?

Stat Tracker Pro connects to official game APIs to pull your real-time and historical match data. Unlike generic trackers, it breaks down your performance at a granular level — showing you not just your overall KD, but your accuracy per weapon, win rate per map, and trend lines across the last 30, 60, or 90 days.


Setup takes under two minutes: activate your key, link your game account via Discord, and your dashboard is live. Data refreshes after every match. Share your profile card with a single link — no account required for viewers.
